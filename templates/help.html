Help - NepaliSaga

1. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What is NepaliSaga? NepaliSaga is an OTT platform designed to bring the best Nepali entertainment, including movies, TV shows, and exclusive originals, directly to your screens.
  • How can I subscribe to NepaliSaga? You can subscribe by signing up for an account, selecting a subscription plan.
  • On which devices can I watch NepaliSaga? NepaliSaga is available on the website. Visit our website for seamless access.
  • How do I change my password? Go to the profile section and click on “Edit Pprofile”. Then click on “change password”.Then you can change your password.
  • Can I watch content offline? No, you can not watch offline. It is not available yet.

2. Subscription

  • Changing Subscription: You can manage your subscription through your account settings. Changes will take effect at the end of your current plan.

3. Technical Support

  • Why is my video not playing? Ensure your internet connection is stable. Clear your browser cache. If the issue persists, contact our support team.
  • What do I do if the site crashes? Refresh the site. Make sure your device’s operating system meets our minimum requirements.

4. Account Management

  • How do I create an account? Click on “Sign Up,” provide your email address, create a password, and verify your email to get started.

5. Content Feedback

  • Where can I provide feedback? We value your feedback! Send us your thoughts via the “Feedback” form or email us at support@nepalisaga.com.
